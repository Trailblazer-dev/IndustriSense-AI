# 📖 IndustriSense AI: User Guide Manual

Welcome to the **IndustriSense AI** operational manual. This guide is designed to help Maintenance Operators, Reliability Engineers, and Plant Managers navigate the platform and leverage predictive intelligence to eliminate unplanned downtime.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Account Creation
IndustriSense AI uses **Corporate Domain Mapping**. 
- To join your organization’s fleet, register using your corporate email (e.g., `engineer@factory-a.com`). 
- You will automatically be grouped with your colleagues and see only your company's machines.
- **Note:** Public domains like Gmail or Yahoo are restricted to ensure enterprise data isolation.

### 1.2 User Roles
- **Maintenance Operator:** Focused on daily health monitoring and executing work orders.
- **Reliability Engineer:** Focused on deep-dive diagnostics, RUL forecasting, and model transparency.
- **Plant Manager:** Focused on fleet-wide health trends, financial ROI, and archiving official audit reports.

---

## 2. The Operational Dashboard

The Dashboard is your "Mission Control" for factory health.

### 2.1 The Health Grid
Each tile represents a unique industrial asset.
- **🔴 CRITICAL (Risk ≥ 75%):** High probability of failure or extreme tool wear. Immediate inspection required.
- **🟡 WARNING (Risk 50-74%):** Elevated stress levels or significant degradation detected. Schedule maintenance soon.
- **🟢 NORMAL (Risk < 50%):** Machine is operating within optimal parameters.

### 2.2 Finding Assets
Use the **Global Filter Bar** at the top of the grid:
- **Search:** Type a Machine ID to find a specific unit.
- **Status Filter:** Quickly isolate only "Critical" or "Warning" assets to prioritize your walk-path.

---

## 3. Diagnostic Intelligence

Clicking any machine tile opens the **Nexus Diagnostic Report**.

### 3.1 Failure Risk %
A unified score combining real-time sensor anomalies with cumulative physical wear.
### 3.2 Tool RUL (Remaining Useful Life)
The estimated number of operating minutes left before the tool reaches its failure threshold. The system also provides a "Calendar Time" estimate (e.g., "14 hours" or "3 days").
### 3.3 Automated Work Orders
For machines in Warning or Critical states, the system automatically generates a **Nexus Work Order** with:
- **Reason Code:** (e.g., "Thermal Delta Mismatch" or "Torque/Speed Interaction").
- **Required Action:** (e.g., "IMMEDIATE SHUTDOWN" or "SCHEDULE INSPECTION").

---

## 4. Reports & Audits

Available for **Production Pro** and **Industrial Nexus** tiers.

### 4.1 Generating an Audit
Navigate to the **Reports** page. The system generates a high-fidelity summary of your fleet's current health, including:
- **Financial Impact:** Estimated savings from prevented failures.
- **Critical Action List:** A prioritized list of machines needing attention.

### 4.2 Archiving
Click **"Confirm Archive"** to save a permanent snapshot of the report. This is used for compliance and historical performance tracking. Your archived reports will appear in the **"Recent Performance Audits"** section at the bottom of the page.

## 5. Licensing & Payments

IndustriSense AI offers flexible, localized industrial licensing.

### 5.1 Pricing Tiers
- **Operational Base:** Monitoring for up to 10 machines.
- **Production Pro:** Adds RUL Forecasting and Audit Archiving.
- **Industrial Nexus:** Unlimited fleet scaling and full system integration.

### 5.2 M-Pesa Checkout
1. Go to the **Pricing** page.
2. Select your desired tier.
3. On the Checkout page, enter your **M-Pesa Phone Number**.
4. Click **"Proceed to Payment"**.
5. You will receive an **STK Push prompt** on your phone.
6. Enter your M-Pesa PIN.
7. Once successful, the dashboard will automatically refresh and unlock your new features.

Need help interpreting a metric? Click the **Floating Bot Icon** in the bottom right of any page. 
- Ask about **"RUL"**, **"Security"**, or **"Pricing"**.
