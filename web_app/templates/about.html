What is IndustriSense AI?

IndustriSense AI is an advanced predictive maintenance ecosystem designed to minimize unplanned downtime. By analyzing real-time sensor telemetry, our system provides operators with actionable insights into machine health, failure probability, and remaining useful life (RUL).

Unlike traditional maintenance schedules, our AI-driven approach allows for Condition-Based Maintenance, ensuring components are only serviced when actually needed—saving costs while increasing safety.

Core Workflow

1. Data Integration

Streaming raw telemetry including temperature, rotational speed, and torque from equipment PLC controllers.

2. Feature Engineering

Transforming raw signals into high-impact interaction features like stress indices and thermal differentials.

3. Neural Inference

Processing data through our parallel XGBoost cores to compute failure risk and degradation rates.

4. Actionable Alerts

Generating prioritized maintenance alerts and RUL countdowns for operational staff.

Critical Considerations
  • Inertial Snapshotting: Predictions reflect current machine state and do not account for future external environmental changes.
  • Class Imbalance: Optimized for minority class (Failure) detection, which may result in higher sensitivity.
  • Verification: AI estimates should always be cross-referenced with physical tool measurements for critical shutdown decisions.
