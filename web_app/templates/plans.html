Deployment & Integration FAQ

How does the AI connect to my hardware?

IndustriSense AI integrates via standard industrial protocols (Modbus, MQTT, OPC-UA). Our Industrial Nexus plan includes on-site assistance to ensure seamless telemetry sync from your existing PLC controllers.

What is the expected ROI?

Most facilities report a 25-35% reduction in unplanned downtime within the first 6 months. By moving to RUL-based maintenance, you eliminate the "replace-on-schedule" waste and prevent emergency part shipping costs.

Is our factory data secure?

Yes. We utilize enterprise-grade AES-256 encryption for data in transit and at rest. Localized edge processing options are available for Industrial Nexus clients who require strict on-premise data residency.
